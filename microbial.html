Microbial Biotechnology

Introduction

Microbial biotechnology uses microorganisms like bacteria and fungi for medicine, industry, and environmental purposes.

Applications

  • Production of antibiotics and vaccines
  • Biofuels and fermentation
  • Bioremediation of polluted environments

Inventions & Discoveries

  • Penicillin production from fungi
  • Recombinant insulin from bacteria
